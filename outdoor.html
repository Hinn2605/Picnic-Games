THE LUXURY PICNIC COMPANY


Kid

To bring fun to outdoor picnics for children, our company offers the following services:
- Outdoor games such as: flashcard, Backyard Obstacle Course, Disc Golf Games,...
- Meals after tiring playtime include: main meal, part milk and part biscuit.
To ensure the safety of children, our company will always have guardians for children who are trained professionals.


Male

For men, our company emphasizes the strength and elegance of men. We bring you a service that is hard to refuse as follows:
- You will have the opportunity to participate in the following competitions to take home prizes: bicycle racing, Ping pong, artificial climbing.
- After tiring outdoor activities they will provide full food during the move.



Female

Women who love lightness and beauty know this, so our company offers you an extremely attractive outdoor service that is:
- Bringing a light competitive game tennis, beach volleyball, DIY Bean Bag Toss,...
- Coming to the break time, you will receive an extremely attractive meal including: Salad, main course and dessert.


Family

Holidays or weekends are days when the family needs a break together to bond with family, understanding that, our company brings you an outdoor service for the family, as follows:
- Bring family bonding games such as: Look at the picture guess the word, Hula Hoop Contest, Family Tennis Match, Homemade Four Square,...
- After tiring activities, they will provide your family with a family meal and an outdoor barbecue.
