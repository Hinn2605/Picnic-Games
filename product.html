Game Recommend

1. KAYAK Boat Game

Kayaking is a big game in camping that you cannot ignore. Kayaking is very interesting, helping team members bond more, showing the winning spirit in the process of rowing to the finish line.

2. FIRE FIGHTER GAME

The torch relay campfire game is a big game in camping that is popular at the start of the campfire. Each group will be given 1 torch and 1 matchbox. Hearing the command of the commander's whistle, our teams quickly lit the torch, starting a jubilant night of camping.

3. Rope GAMES

How to play this game is as follows, the organizers have prepared suspension bridges made of ropes, the player's job is to cross the bridge in the shortest time. You can play in groups and the team that completes the game first is the winner. Depending on the chosen location, you will experience different challenges of rope games in the forest or on the water.

4. FIGHT RATING GAMES

This is one of the popular folk games, loved by many people. The items that need to be prepared for this game are the flags. The teams will stand in a vertical line and 10m - 15m away from the flag place. When playing, the members have to bow, rotate the trunk 10 times and run up to steal the flag before they can run back. Do this until the last person.

5. DRAGON GAME

The equipment to be prepared is a long rope The organizers draw a dividing line on the ground, each team will hold one end of the rope. At the command of the commander, the teams that will use all their force to pull the leader of the other team across the line, will win. During the game, the cheers are when the team's solidarity becomes stronger

6. BUILDING GAMES

The wooden puzzle game is both intellectual and requires high ingenuity, promising to be one of the exciting camping games that you cannot miss.
